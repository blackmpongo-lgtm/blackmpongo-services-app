# Appointment Booking Guide

## Overview
This document provides a comprehensive guide to the appointment booking feature of the application, detailing the availability, selection process, and cancellation policy.

## Availability
- **Days Available**: Appointments can only be booked on Sundays.  
- **Hours**: Specify the available hours for booking on Sundays.

## Official Selection Process
1. Users can select an appointment slot from the available slots shown in the application.
2. After selection, users are required to confirm their appointment by clicking the 'Confirm' button.
3. A confirmation email will be sent to the user after successful booking.

## Cancellation Policy
- **Cancellation Window**: Users may cancel their appointments up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any penalty.  
- **Late Cancellations**: Cancellations made less than 24 hours prior to the appointment time will incur a fee of 50% of the appointment cost.
- **No-Show Policy**: Failure to attend the appointment without prior cancellation will result in the full appointment fee being charged.

## Contact Information
For further queries related to appointments, please contact customer support at support@example.com.
